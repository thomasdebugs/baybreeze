Castle Skins Tier List

10 min read
Kingdom Foundations
Updated: Oct 2025

Table of Contents

  • A Tier Skins

Castle skins are more than just cosmetic flair; they provide powerful "owned" and "using" attributes that can significantly boost your kingdom's power. This guide breaks down the best skins by tier to help you prioritize your collection and usage.

S+ Tier Skins

The absolute best all-around value. Excellent owned/using attributes and powerful active skills.

Skin Name Best For Key Features & Skills
Cloud Castle All Players Must-have. One of the few ways to increase your hero level cap. Used primarily for this "owned" attribute rather than its active skill.
Tower of Chaos Horde Top choice for Horde. Significant boost to soldier ATK/HP and Horde march size. Skill boosts PvP damage and march speed.
Nightfall Nature Best option for Nature. Massive "owned" attributes for the faction: soldier ATK, HP, and march size.
Genesis Mythic The only Mythic skin. Powerful universal owned attributes. Unique "Domain" skill buffs nearby guild members and increases enemy casualties.

S Tier Skins

Very powerful and excellent choices for your primary active skin.

Skin Name Best For Key Features & Skills
Blazing Fortress Horde Rare hero critical rate bonus. Powerful skill buffs both you and guild members.
Temple of Soul PvP Top-tier PvP skin. Increases march size and dramatically increases enemy wounded ratio.
Shell Castle Nature Offers a hero healing rate bonus. Skill buffs both you and guild members.
Steel Citadel Offense Excellent offensive skin. Significant attack bonus when attacking others. Massively increases march size.
Mountain Prime League / Defense Defensive powerhouse. Its ability to be leveled up gives it an edge over many A-tier skins.

A Tier Skins

Good "owned" attributes. Can be used as active skins if S-Tier options are unavailable.

Skin Name Category Key Features & Skills
Dragon Temple Active Skin Good for Horde players, offering a hero dodge bonus.
Fantasy Castle Active Skin Beneficial for League due to skill damage bonus.
Christmas Castle Active Skin Offers a rare critical damage bonus.
Cherry Blossom Active Skin Provides a rare critical rate bonus.
Unbeatable Glory Owned Skin One of the best A-tier owned skins. March size increase + bonuses to all soldier ATK/HP.
Mobile Fortress Owned Skin Good for League. Bonuses to march size, hospital capacity, and soldier capacity.
Lucky Turkey Owned Skin Fun skill: drops Thanksgiving feasts for guild members to gather.

B Tier Skins

Skin Name Best For Notes
Griffin League Easy-to-obtain early game skin.
Skybound League Good "owned" skin offering HP and march size increases.
Fantasy Carousel Nature Decent choice due to unique summoned unit HP bonus.

C & D Tier Skins

Low Priority

These skins generally have less impactful attributes or are purely for economic/PvE utility. Not recommended for active PvP slots.

Tier Skin Name Key Utility
C Kanan Bastion Decent early-game soldier training speed bonus.
D Dreamlike Stage Increases ranch yield (Economic).
D Iron Anvil Fort Boosts timber and stone gathering (Economic).
D Crystal Fort Damage reduction bonus in Adventure mode (PvE).
