Hero and Resource Management

Effective hero and resource management is the foundation of success in Top Heroes. Making smart decisions early will save you countless hours and resources down the line. Understanding how to allocate your resources strategically can mean the difference between a powerful hero queue and a scattered, ineffective team.

Critical Early Game Decision

Focus on One Faction: At the start, choose one of the three main factions (League, Horde, or Nature) and concentrate all your resources there. This allows you to build a powerful, cohesive team more quickly than spreading yourself thin. Faction synergies and bonuses only apply when heroes belong to the same faction, making this focus even more crucial.

Core Management Principles

  • Focus Your Resources: It's better to have one strong, fully-invested hero queue than several mediocre ones. Concentrate your gear, shards, and other resources on your main team to get the most power out of them. Every resource spent on secondary queues delays your primary team's progression.
  • Soldiers are a Must: Don't neglect your soldiers! They are a significant part of your team's overall power and are often the difference between winning and losing a fight. Always ensure your heroes are accompanied by the maximum number of soldiers.
  • Save Universal Legendary Shards: Universal Legendary Hero Shards are precious. Do not use them on Legendary heroes. Instead, save them to upgrade your Mythic heroes, who are much stronger in the long run and harder to acquire.
  • Use Meat for Your Main Queue: Be mindful of how you spend your meat resource. Focus on your main hero queue to ensure they have enough power to advance. For Blue/Purple heroes, don't spend meat past level 110-120 as they'll be replaced by better heroes soon.

Pro Tip: Synergy is Key

Read Hero Abilities: Don't just pick heroes based on rarity. Read their skills and passive abilities to build strong, synergistic teams. For example, some heroes gain damage from attacking 'burned' enemies, so pair them with heroes who can inflict that status effect. Also pay close attention to Hero bonding for additional stat bonuses and powerful combined effects.

Resource Gathering Priority

Gather Rubies: Rubies are a crucial bottleneck in the late game. Actively mine for rubies on the world map and assign your strongest heroes to the ruby mine back at your home base. Ruby production speed becomes increasingly important as you progress through hero upgrades.

Faction Guide: Choosing Your Path

This guide is based on deep community analysis and will help you understand the core mechanics and strengths of each faction. Choosing your main faction early is one of the most important decisions you'll make! Each faction has unique strengths, weaknesses, and playstyles that cater to different strategic preferences.

League Faction

Tactical Precision & Skill Damage Masters

League is a strong faction that focuses heavily on skill damage and strategic positioning. It's relatively easy to acquire the meta heroes early on, but they can be expensive to fully awaken. League excels in controlled, methodical combat where timing and skill rotation matter most.

Meta Heroes

Adjudicator: Core tank and frontline defender with exceptional durability
Rose Princess: Essential support and damage dealer with healing capabilities
Paragon: High burst damage dealer specializing in skill-based attacks
Artificer: Late-game powerhouse with devastating area effects

Other Key Heroes: Bard provides crucial buffs and crowd control, Pyromancer (Pyro) serves as a flagship DPS character with consistent damage output, and Nun offers powerful support capabilities. Pyro is highlighted as one of the most reliable damage dealers throughout the game's progression.

League Weaknesses

Struggles against the high sustained DPS output of Horde teams. Can be expensive to fully awaken, requiring up to 27 Soul Stones for a three-Legendary lineup. The faction's reliance on skill timing can be challenging for new players to master effectively.

Legendary Heroes (Early Game)

  • Astrologer: Strong early game hero, will be replaced by Paragon around week 6
  • Secret Keeper: Solid support hero, replaced by Rose Princess later
  • Bard: Versatile buffer, eventually replaced by Bishop (though some players keep Bard)
  • Pyromancer: Continues into mid-game with excellent value, replaced by Artificer in late game

Mythic Heroes (Long-term Investment)

Mythic heroes start arriving in week six and represent your endgame team composition:

  • Paragon → Replaces Astrologer as primary burst damage dealer
  • Rose Princess → Replaces Secret Keeper with superior healing and damage
  • Bishop → Replaces Bard with enhanced buffing capabilities
  • Artificer → Replaces Pyromancer as the ultimate damage dealer

League Awakening Priority

Legendary awakening order: Pyro (2+1) first for immediate power spike, Adjudicator (Max) second for frontline stability, Nun (2+1) third for support, then finish Pyro completely, finish Nun, and finally invest in Bard.

Note: Some endgame players prefer using Bard and Pyromancer over Bishop and Nun due to their reliable performance and lower awakening costs.

Final Lineup Configuration:

  • Front Row: Adjudicator & Rose Princess (tank and support/healer)
  • Middle Row: Nun & (Bard or Bishop) (support and buffers)
  • Back Row: Artificer & Paragon (primary damage dealers)

Nature Faction

Survivability, Sustain & Summon Specialists

Nature is all about survivability and sustain. They use minions and summons to absorb damage, which allows them to stay in fights longer and makes them a very balanced faction in the counter system. Nature heroes excel at outlasting opponents through healing, shields, and tactical summons that protect key heroes.

Meta Heroes

Tidecaller: Best hero in the game - combines DPS, healing, and tankiness
Monk: Reliable tank and frontline anchor with excellent survivability
Ne Zha: Powerful damage dealer with high burst potential
Pixie: Support and crowd control specialist

Nature Pro-Tip

The Nightfall Castle skin provides powerful buffs specifically for Nature heroes, offering significant stat boosts and faction bonuses. This is a major strategic advantage that Nature players should prioritize acquiring!

Legendary Heroes (Early Game)

Nature only has two initial Mythic heroes, so you'll rely on these legendary heroes for a longer period compared to other factions:

  • Treeguard: Solid early tank, will be replaced by Monk
  • Druid: Versatile support, replaced by the exceptional Tidecaller
  • Windwalker, Stonemason, Sage: All useful in early-mid game progression
  • Stonemason: Available in S1 Adventure Mode (optional but recommended)

Mythic Heroes (Long-term Investment)

  • Monk → Replaces Treeguard as the premier tank
  • Tidecaller → Replaces Druid and becomes the faction cornerstone
  • Petalis → Replaces Sage with enhanced support capabilities
  • Ne Zha → Replaces Windwalker as primary damage dealer

Nature Priorities

Legendary awakening order: Pixie (2+1) for crowd control, Forest Maiden (2+1) for damage, then Sage for support capabilities.

Exclusive gear priority: Focus Tidecaller and Ne Zha (if available) to +10 first for maximum impact. These two heroes carry the faction's damage potential.

Hero focus order: Tidecaller is absolute priority, then Pixie, Forest Maiden, Monk, and finally Sage.

Final Lineup Configuration:

  • Front Row: Ne Zha & Monk (damage dealer and tank)
  • Middle Row: Petalis & Tidecaller (support and all-rounder)
  • Back Row: Pixie & Forest Maiden (crowd control and ranged DPS)

Horde Faction

Raw Power & Relentless Aggression

Horde is the master of raw attack and high sustained damage output. This faction is unmatched in PvE performance and can destroy League teams even with a significant power disadvantage in PvP due to their overwhelming offensive capabilities. Horde's aggressive playstyle rewards players who prefer all-out assault strategies.

Meta Heroes

Beastmaster: Core tank with exceptional frontline presence
Wukong: Essential high-damage dealer with game-changing abilities
Desert Prince: Frontline warrior with strong offensive capabilities
Witch: Crowd control specialist with debuffing abilities

The Blazing Fortress and Tower of Chaos castle skins provide powerful attack and critical rate bonuses specifically for Horde heroes, amplifying their already impressive damage output.

Cheapest to max out: You only need to awaken one Legendary hero fully, costing just 9 Soul Stones! This makes Horde the most cost-effective faction for F2P players.

  • Barbarian: Particularly effective against Nature faction due to anti-healing mechanics
  • Soulmancer: Versatile performer, strong against both League and Horde factions
  • Warlock: Best choice for maximizing PvE damage rankings and boss fights

Awakening priority: Soulmancer or Barbarian to (4+1) depending on your focus, with Warlock as secondary investment for PvE content.

Obtaining and Using Hero Shards

Hero shards are the lifeblood of your progression system. Understanding where to acquire them and how to use them efficiently is crucial for long-term success. Poor shard management in early game can set you back weeks or even months.

How to Obtain Hero Shards

  • Adventure Mode: Progressing through the main campaign provides a steady stream of faction-specific shards.
  • Daily Tasks & Bounties: Completing your daily goals and bounty missions consistently rewards hero shards.
  • Events: Participating in special events is one of the best ways to earn large quantities of shards, including universal ones.
  • Tavern: Recruiting new heroes from the tavern can yield both full heroes and valuable shards.
  • Plundering Carriages: A lucrative daily method for acquiring shards by raiding other players' resources.
  • Battlefield: Guild performance in the weekly Battlefield event earns significant shard rewards for all members.

Blue Hero Shards

Blue shards are most common in the early game. Here's how to maximize their value:

Best Uses for Blue Shards

Leveling Up: Your primary focus - max out your blue heroes to unlock their passive traits.
Trade Store (Recommended): Exchange for epic shards (20 blue = 1 epic, 10x weekly limit).
Hero Tower: After maxing your blue heroes, donate excess shards to the Hero Tower to boost your faction-wide stats (unlocks at level 22).

Epic Hero Shards

Epic shards require more strategic planning. Don't spend them randomly!

Prioritize Unique Traits First

Before exchanging epic shards, max out these essential unique traits that provide powerful, game-wide bonuses. These bonuses will accelerate your progress significantly.

Brawler: 30% Tech Research Speed
Knight: 30% Soldier Training Speed
Ranger: 30% Ruby Gathering Speed

Legendary & Mythic Hero Shards

Critical: Save Universal Shards

Universal Shards are your most valuable resource. Be extremely selective with them. Community wisdom strongly suggests saving these exclusively for mythic heroes, as their shards are much harder to obtain.

If you absolutely must use them on legendaries: Only invest in top-tier heroes that will be a long-term part of your team (like Pyromancer or Beastmaster). Wasting universal shards on temporary heroes is a mistake you will regret later.

Building Hero Queue Power

Improving your hero queue power is a multi-faceted process that involves strategic hero upgrades, smart gear management, and overall account progression. A core strategy is to focus your resources on a single faction to build a powerful main queue that benefits from faction bonuses and specialized gear.

1. Hero Upgrade Priority

  • Focus on a Single Faction: This allows you to create a powerful main queue with faction bonuses and specialized gear.
  • Prioritize Your Main Queue: Don't spread your resources too thin. Focus on maxing out your main team of heroes before moving on to others.
  • Key Hero Priorities:
    • Tanks First: Max out your main tank (like Adjudicator or Beastmaster) early. Their survival is key to protecting your damage dealers.
    • Damage Dealers: Heroes like Wukong, Paragon, and Storm Maiden are great investments after your tank is solid.
    • F2P Staples: Tidecaller and Monk are often high-priority upgrades for free-to-play players due to their incredible value and versatility.
  • Read Abilities: Pay close attention to hero abilities and passive skills to find powerful synergies within your chosen faction.

2. Gear Upgrade Suggestions

  • Epic > Legendary (at first): A fully upgraded Epic faction gear set (+10) is often stronger than a base-level Legendary piece. Focus on maxing your Epic set first.
  • Upgrade Path: Get Epic gear to +10, then start saving resources for Legendary gear. A base Legendary piece is an instant upgrade from a +10 Epic piece.
  • Keep Sets Leveled: Try to keep your four gear sets within the same upgrade tier (e.g., all between +10 and +20) to maintain balanced stat bonuses.
  • Faction Gear: Always aim for gear with faction bonuses from the guild store for a significant, targeted power boost.
  • Mythic Gear: This is an extremely long-term goal. Only consider it after your Legendary gear is completely maxed out.

3. General Power Improvement Tips

  • Research: Technology research provides significant passive bonuses to your heroes and your entire army. Never let your research lab sit idle.
  • Buildings & Statues: Upgrading your buildings and faction statues directly increases your power level and provides various combat and economic benefits.
  • Training Soldiers: A hero queue is only as strong as the army behind it. Regularly train soldiers to keep your army at maximum capacity.
  • Join a Strong Guild: A good guild gives you access to valuable resources, rewards from guild events, and crucial support from experienced players.
  • Morale System: Understand how the morale system works in combat. High morale can significantly increase your damage output and turn the tide of a battle.
