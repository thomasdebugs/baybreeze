Good Investments

Best Value for Diamonds

These are the best ways to spend your diamonds for maximum value. Prioritize these purchases and events.

  • Events: Spend on Wheel, Dice, and Gacha events to unlock mythic heroes, Ultimate Weapons, and legendary decor.
  • Egg Bounties: Purchase additional bounties every day (2 per day, 200 diamonds each).
  • Guild Race: Buy task tickets to earn more guild honor.
  • VIP Store: Regularly purchase silver chests, stamina, speed-ups, and pet food.
  • Guild Tech: Donate to guild traits to boost the entire guild's power.

Situational Investments

Use Sparingly

These investments can be useful, but you should only spend diamonds on them if the situation calls for it and you have a specific goal in mind.

  • Refreshing carriages for hero shards.
  • Buying a relic scroll from the VIP store.
  • Buying resources from the VIP store (only if you are critically low for a major upgrade).
  • Purchasing the temporary additional construction queue.

Bad Investments

Avoid at All Costs

Avoid these investments as they are an extremely poor use of your valuable diamonds.

  • Recruiting from the tavern without tickets. Farm these in adventure mode and from daily tasks instead.
  • Wishing for relics without scrolls.
  • Refreshing the ruin hunt.
  • Speeding up construction, research, or training directly with diamonds.
  • Refreshing guild race tasks.
