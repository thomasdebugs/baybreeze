Season 1: Shadow of the World Tree

Season 1 introduces new mechanics, resources, and events that fundamentally change the gameplay. Hereâ€™s what you need to know to succeed.

Key Changes & New Resources

  • New Resource - Water: Essential for progressing through the season's adventure map. You cannot advance without it.
  • The Spring Building: You will get a new building, the Spring, which generates Water over time. Upgrading the Spring is a top priority to increase your water production and storage.
  • Sandstorms: A new map feature that can impact marches and battles. Be mindful of their location and effects. Make sure to donate water to your guild to help combat the effects!

Main Events & Tasks

  • Dune Devourer: A recurring world boss event. Participating is crucial as it's a major source of branches and currency for the Season 1 shop.
  • Pet Rush: This event gives points for using pet food. It's wise to save your pet food for this event to maximize rewards.
  • Kingdom vs Kingdom (KvK): As always, save your speed-ups, shards, and other resources for the daily tasks during KvK.

Strategic Priorities & Tips

Prioritize S1 Research

The Season 1 research tree offers powerful, permanent bonuses that persist after the season ends. This should be your absolute top priority. Focus on nodes that boost your S1 resource generation and combat stats first.

Rush Adventure Mode Gates

In the Season 1 adventure mode, your main goal should be to use your Water to unlock gates as quickly as possible. You can bypass and ignore the bosses until much later to progress faster and unlock more of the map. The bosses hit hard!

Upgrade Your Spring: Leveling up your Spring building is critical. The rewards for a high-level spring (up to level 35 is recommended for max rewards) are significant, and the decorations it provides carry over to Season 2, giving you a head start.

Season 2: Ember of the Frozen Flame

Season 2 builds on the mechanics of the first season while introducing new challenges, resources, and a strong emphasis on single-faction teams.

Key Changes & New Mechanics

  • The Furnace: This new building replaces the Spring from Season 1. Your primary goal is to keep the furnace active and upgrade it.
  • Blizzards: Instead of Sandstorms, the map is now affected by Blizzards, which introduce new environmental challenges.
  • Frost Fire Cores: This is the primary currency for Season 2, earned by completing objectives and milestones.
  • Snow Plundering: You can now plunder snow on the map, a new micro-game that can yield hidden rewards. This unlocks when your guild captures ruins, providing 10 attempts per day.

Ember of the Frozen Flame Shop Priorities

Shop Smart: Permanent Buffs First!

How you spend your Frost Fire Cores is critical. The consensus strategy is to prioritize permanent buffs first.

  1. Decorations First: Your absolute first priority should be to purchase all decorations from the shop. These provide permanent, account-wide buffs to march size, soldier HP, and soldier attack.
  2. Dragon Essence: After securing all decorations, Dragon Essence becomes the next priority. This resource is essential for upgrading your buildings to the new "Brilliance" levels (Castle Level 41+).

Strategic Priorities & Tips

  • High-Temp Furnace: Level this up in your castle. You can also put this into "overdrive" to drive up your damage buffs against world bosses.
  • Iron Workshop: This is a seasonal building that will generate iron for you, just like your other resource buildings.
  • World Bosses: Legion Boss gets a boost to level 9, Frost Titan is the S2 version of King of Bones, and Frostbone Dragon replaces Dune Devourer with the same mechanics.
  • New Tech Tree: Prioritize the new S2 research tree as it offers significant bonuses, including a crucial +5 to your max hero level.

General Season 2 FAQ

Does S2 have the 4 Nationals event?No.
Did my server group increase in S2?Yes, the server group is now 6 instead of 4 (may vary by grouping).
What is the event schedule for S2?GR > GAR > GR > KvK > GR > GAR > GR > KvK. It's a grind!
Does S2 Research tree stay after S2 ends?Yes, you can continue to work on the research tree after S2 ends.
Why can't I build the Iron Furnace or Workshop?You need to be Castle Level 35 before these buildings will unlock.
I'm having issues in Adventure mode!Get your personal furnace temp to 30 degrees by leveling it up. Only your personal furnace counts, not the guild furnace.
What does the furnace overdrive do?It boosts your temp for a period of time. You can use this boost 5 times per day and receive additionaly boosts from guild members.
How do I unlock the 7th Lord Gear?It unlocks 78 days into Season 2, but your other runes must be Mythic first.
How do I acquire Coal & Iron?Iron Workshop, event rewards, killing Frost Titans, seasonal milestones, adventure mode, capturing ruins, and melting snow.
How do I acquire Obsidian?Seasonal Carriages, World Boss ranking rewards, owning ruins, and daily Seasonal Arena rankings.
How do I get kingdom emblems (S2 Currency)?Seasonal ranking rewards, Citadel Clash events, and Peak Ranking Match (3v3).
What does Pet Essence do?Lets you level pets to 120 so you can promote them to Mythic.
How do I melt snow on a tile?When a ruin is captured, nearby snow melts. You can then only melt snow on tiles connected to already-melted ground by sending scouts.
Can I get Flickerkit from melting snow?No.
Do I get pets from saving them from snow?Not directly, but you can get green eggs for saving frozen pets.
How can I get free Flickerkit in Season 2?Through guild progress in the Snow Melt Event and from completing personal milestones.
Can other players take frozen villagers, tiger bosses, or frozen pets I find?Yes.
Does melted snow come back after a blizzard?No.
