Tips for Free-to-Play (F2P) Players

Core F2P Principles

  • Patience is Key: You won't be able to compete with players who spend a lot of money, and that's okay. The F2P strategy is about delayed gratification and maximizing your resources over time.
  • Take Part in All Guild Activities: An active guild is your best friend. Participate in rallies, guild bosses, and other guild events to earn a consistent stream of rewards and support.

Resource Management

  • Save Diamonds for Events: Save your diamonds specifically for the Dice and Wheel events to get Mythic heroes and other rare items. Avoid spending them on small daily and weekly store items.
  • Save Universal Legendary Shards: Universal Legendary Hero Shards are precious. Do not use them on Legendary heroes. Instead, save them to upgrade your Mythic heroes, who are much stronger in the long run.
  • Use Free Resources Wisely: The game gives you a ton of free items. Prioritize spending your diamonds on useful things like the second construction queue or event-specific gold chests. Skip the fancy cosmetics or unnecessary items.

Progression Priorities

  • Don't Overextend: Focus all of your resources on one primary hero queue. Don't waste valuable gear, shards, or experience on secondary queues. Use "hand-me-down" gear for your other teams and let them catch up naturally.
  • Prioritize Construction: Your Castle is the most important building. Its level determines the max level of other key structures like the Hospital, Research Cottage, Guild Hall, and Training Grounds. Always keep a builder working.
  • Prioritize Your Faction's Statues: When building, focus on the statues for your main faction's barracks. This provides a significant boost to your march size and overall troop power.

Spending Real Money: Worthwhile Investments

For Low-Spenders

If you are going to spend a small amount of money, these one-time or low-cost purchases will provide the best value and make your leveling experience much more enjoyable.

High-Value Early Purchases:

  • Pyro Hero Unlock: $1.99
  • Permanent Privilege: $9.99 (from the store)
  • Monthly Pass: $14.99/month
  • Construction & Research Queues: $1.99 - $9.99

Good Value One-Time Fund Packs:

Once you reach a higher castle level, these packs in the store offer good value but are not required. They range from $9.99 to $19.99.

  • Adventure Fund
  • Construction Fund
  • Power Fund
  • Growth Fund
