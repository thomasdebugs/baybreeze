Tower of Light & Darkness Guide

5 min read
Kingdom Foundations
Updated: Oct 2025

Table of Contents

  • Crucial Community Etiquette

Unlocked via the Lord's Hall at level 21, the Towers of Light and Darkness are deployable structures that provide powerful, temporary buffs and debuffs on the world map. Using them correctly is a key strategic element for any guild.

Tower of Light (Buff)

The Tower of Light provides a powerful buff to all guild members who are within its radius. This buff only affects your guild.

Coordinate with Your Guild!

Make sure to communicate with your guild mates before deploying a tower. The tower buffs do not stack. If you drop a level 3 tower and someone else drops a level 5 tower, the level 3 one will be obsolete and its resources wasted.

Strategic Uses:

  • World Bosses: Place it near a world boss (like the Golem or Slime Monster) to give your guild a significant damage advantage.
  • Guild Events: Use it during guild-specific events to boost performance and secure higher rankings.
  • Defensive Support: Place it within your guild's hive to strengthen allies defending against an attack, giving them a crucial edge.

Tower of Darkness (Debuff)

The Tower of Darkness applies a powerful debuff to all non-friendly units in its radius. This means it weakens every player who is not in your guild, making it a potent tool for area denial and defense.

Strategic Uses:

  • Territory Defense: This is the absolute best use for the tower. Place it near your guild's territory during KvK or other server-wide wars to weaken incoming attackers and make your defenses much harder to break.
  • Controlling Choke Points: Deploy it at key strategic locations on the map, like narrow passages or important resource nodes, to hinder enemy movements and create tactical disadvantages for your opponents.

Crucial Community Etiquette

A Major Rule to Follow

NEVER place a Tower of Darkness on a public world boss. Since it debuffs all other guilds, it is considered a toxic and hostile move that will likely result in your entire guild being targeted by other alliances. Other guilds will often destroy these towers on sight.

Always use the Tower of Light for public, cooperative objectives.
