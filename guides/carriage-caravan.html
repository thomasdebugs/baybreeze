A Player's Guide to the Carriage Caravan

7 min read
Kingdom Foundations
Updated: Oct 2025

The Carriage Caravan is one of the most important and profitable features in Top Heroes, offering a fantastic way to earn valuable items. This guide will walk you through how it works, why sharing is always the best choice, and how to protect yourself and your guild.

Accessing the Caravan

Before you can access the carriage caravan, you must have a Guild Hall built in your city. Once you have it, you can access the feature by clicking on the carriage building or its icon on the world map.

The interface is simple and has two main sections:

  • Your Carriages: This is where you can see your own carriages. You are allowed to deploy a carriage four times a day.
  • Other's Carriages: This is where you can find other players' carriages to either help them or, if you choose the dark path, plunder them. You are allowed to plunder up to four times a day.

Understanding Carriages and Rarity

The quality of carriages you deploy directly impacts the value of the rewards you receive. Carriages have various quality levels, ranging from Common all the way up to Mythic.

  • Red Mythic Carriages are the most valuable and are sought after by every player.
  • You can re-roll your carriage quality for a better one once a day for free.
  • You can also use vouchers to re-roll and try for a better quality carriage. Once you hit the Mythic tier, it locks, so you won't accidentally re-roll past it!
  • A Premium Carriage Voucher, available at VIP 10, guarantees a Mythic-quality carriage. You can purchase one once a day for 2000 Diamonds.

The Golden Rule: Share, Don't Plunder

Always Share, Never Plunder

This is the most crucial part of the game's carriage feature. While it might be tempting to hit that plunder button, doing so is highly discouraged and can lead to major consequences within your guild and server community.

The Benefits of Sharing

When you have a high-quality carriage, especially a Mythic one, the best thing you can do is hit the Share button. This sends an invitation to your friends, guildmates, and public chat members to help you defend your carriage.

  • When they join, everyone gets a share of the rewards!
  • If your carriage successfully fends off all attackers, the owner (you) gets all the rewards without any being divided.

The Consequences of Plundering

A Criminal Offense

Plundering another player's carriage is considered a criminal offense within the game's community. The rewards from a plunder are divided among up to five players, and youâ€™re actively harming another player's progress. On most servers, plundering a Mythic or Guild carriage is seen as particularly damaging to the server's collective growth and breaks community trust.

Non-Aggression Pacts (NAP)

Because of the problems caused by plundering, most guilds and servers have established Non-Aggression Pacts (NAP). These are agreements to not attack each other's carriages. Guilds often have dedicated group chats where players can share screenshots and details of anyone who breaks the pact.

How to Participate & Protect Yourself

  • Join a guild: This is the best way to get involved in server-wide NAPs and have a network of players to help defend you.
  • Be a good community member: Help defend your guildmates' carriages, and they will help you in return. Teamwork is essential.
  • Expose wrongdoers: If you are a victim of plundering, be sure to take screenshots and report them to your guild leaders to enforce the NAP.

By working together, your server can grow stronger, and everyone can benefit from the incredible rewards offered by the carriage caravan!
