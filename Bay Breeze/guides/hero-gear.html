Optimizing your heroes' gear is one of the most impactful ways to maximize their combat effectiveness. Arming your heroes with the right equipment can dramatically alter battle outcomes. This guide is based on an in-depth analysis of recommended gear sets—Fury of Blood, Glory of the Knight, and Titan's Might—across the Nature, League, and Horde factions.

Experimentation is Encouraged!

Please note that there are many different configurations, options, and theories when it comes to hero gear. Once the conversion system arrives on your server, you'll have 34 days to convert your gear multiple times without cost. We encourage you to do your own research, but you can use this guide as a powerful starting point for your builds.

Understanding the Gear Sets

Each of the three main gear sets provides a distinct advantage tailored to specific hero roles and skill mechanics. Choosing the right set is about amplifying what a hero already does best.

Gear Set Primary Benefit Focus Typical Hero Role
Fury of Blood Tankiness, HP, and Self-Sustain. Scales with Max HP. Tanks and HP-Scaling Supports.
Glory of the Knight Damage Output and Attack Power. Scales with Attack Power. Damage Dealers.
Titan's Might Niche Scaling & Specialized Utility. For unique skill interactions. Niche Damage Dealers.

Hero Recommended Gear Rationale

Soulmancer Fury of Blood (Tank)
Glory of the Knight (DPS)		 Choose Fury for utility and tanking, or Glory for damage contribution via Attack Power.

Final Rule: Check Your Hero's Skill Scaling!

The Golden Rule of Gearing

When in doubt, always read your hero's skill descriptions. The text will tell you exactly what you need to know.

  • If the skill text says damage, healing, or shields scale with Max HP, always choose Fury of Blood.
  • If it scales with Attack Power, your first and best choice is almost always Glory of the Knight!
