Introduction to Brilliance Levels

Congratulations on reaching Castle Level 40! You've just unlocked the Brilliance Level system, the ultimate progression path designed to continually enhance your heroes and soldiers, paving the way for higher-tier troops and dominating the battlefield.

Brilliance Levels represent the pinnacle of your castle's development, pushing your growth to Castle Level 65 and beyond.

Key Brilliance Unlocks:

  • New Max Hero Level: Your heroes can now advance to Level 220.
  • The Brilliance Institute: Unlocked at Brilliance Level 2, this is your primary engine for end-game power, granting massive stat boosts and research for troop upgrades.
  • Core Resources: Dragon Essence, Wood, and Rubies become the most critical currencies for these advanced upgrades.

Dragon Essence: The End-Game Powerhouse

Dragon Essence is the rarest and most critical resource in the Brilliance system, used exclusively for high-end research and building upgrades. Mastering its acquisition is key to rapid progression.

Source Type Acquisition Methods Focus / Priority
Consistent Income Daily Bounties, Void Vault completion. Reliable, low-volume, daily collection.
Trading & Stores Guild Honor Store (up to 35/week), Trade Store (weekly), Season Store purchases. Prioritize weekly purchases using currency and honor.
Production (S2) Essence Workshop conversion of raw materials (Wood, Stone, Meat, Crystals). HIGH PRIORITY: Level the Workshop to maximize conversion attempts.
Events & Packs Citadel Clash, Rush Events, limited-time celebrations, and direct purchases. Save for events; they provide the largest bulk rewards.

Brilliance Leveling Strategy & Priorities

The overarching strategy is to rush Castle Brilliance Level 2 (CBL 2) to unlock the Brilliance Institute as quickly as possible.

Phase 1: Rush to Castle Brilliance Level 2 (CL 50)

CL 41 - 45 126 per level (Total: 630) Research Cottage Lvl 40, Hospital Lvl 40 Castle Brilliance Lvl 1
CL 46 - 50 151 per level (Total: 755) League Barracks BL 1, Guild Brilliance Lvl 1 Castle Brilliance Lvl 2

Ultimate Goal: Level 11 Troops

The primary objective of the CBL 2 rush is to reach the Horde/Nature/League/Order Soldier Level +1 research inside the Brilliance Institute. This is the only way to gain Level 11 troops, which provides a monumental leap in combat power!

Pro-Tips for Efficient Progression

Key Strategies

  • Resource Stockpiling: Start saving Wood and Rubies early. Do not spend them frivolously as you approach CL 40.
  • Speed-Ups: Save your Universal Speed-Ups for the extremely long build times (up to 30 days) and extensive research in the Brilliance Institute.
  • Guild Focus: An active guild is paramount. Guild members can speed up your long builds, and the Guild Honor store provides guaranteed Dragon Essence income.
  • Targeted Upgrades: Always level your Main Faction Barracks before other faction Barracks to get the maximum power increase for your primary fighting troops.
